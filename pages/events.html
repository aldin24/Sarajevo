Recommended events



Sarajevo Film Festival

The Sarajevo Film Festival offers audiences a chance to see the most interesting new films and provides an opportunity for regional directors to network and promote their films, while aspiring film makers have a chance to gain new knowledge and experience. Today, SFF is a must-see on the world map of film festivals and serves as the most significant place for film in Southeastern Europe.




Sarajevo Holiday Market

Sarajevo Holiday Market makes the holiday period in Sarajevo more attractive and marks the Catholic Christmas and New Year and the Orthodox Christmas and New Year by offering an assortment of local products.
